# ShieldAI Kenya

## Detect. Verify. Protect.

ShieldAI Kenya is an AI-powered digital safety platform designed to combat online scams, cyber fraud, misinformation and unsafe digital practices in Kenya. The platform combines scam detection, website verification, phone number reputation checks, AI education and cybersecurity awareness into one secure ecosystem.

Our mission is to empower individuals, businesses, students and communities with the knowledge and tools needed to stay safe online while promoting responsible and ethical use of Artificial Intelligence.


## Problem Statement

Cybercrime and online scams continue to affect thousands of people across Kenya every year. Common threats include:

* M-Pesa fraud and reversal scams
* Fake job opportunities
* Phishing emails and websites
* Social media impersonation
* Investment and cryptocurrency scams
* Identity theft
* AI-generated misinformation

Many victims lack access to reliable verification tools and digital literacy resources.

ShieldAI Kenya addresses this challenge by providing real-time scam detection, educational resources and community-driven reporting mechanisms.

## Key Features

### AI Scam Detector

Users can submit suspicious:

* SMS messages
* WhatsApp messages
* Emails
* Social media messages
* Job advertisements

The system analyzes content and generates a Scam Shield Score indicating the likelihood of fraud.


### Phone Number Verification

Users can search phone numbers and view:

* Scam reports
* Risk ratings
* Community feedback
* Report frequency


### Website Verification

Users can verify websites by checking:

* Domain reputation
* HTTPS security
* Domain age
* Potential phishing indicators


### Scam Reporting Center

Community members can report:

* Scam phone numbers
* Fraudulent websites
* Suspicious messages
* Fake job offers
* Online fraud incidents


### AI Academy

An interactive learning platform covering:

* Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
* Prompt Engineering
* Cybersecurity Basics
* Scam Awareness
* Ethical AI
* Digital Safety


### Harmful Search Prevention

ShieldAI discourages searches related to:

* Fraud
* Scamming techniques
* Cybercrime
* Illegal hacking

Instead, users are redirected to educational and ethical learning resources.


### Scam Alerts Dashboard

Displays:

* Trending scams
* New cyber threats
* Community reports
* Safety advisories


### AI Assistant

An intelligent assistant that helps users:

* Understand scams
* Learn cybersecurity concepts
* Explore AI safely
* Access educational content
